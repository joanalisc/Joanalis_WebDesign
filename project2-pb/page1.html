...6 Friends Just Trying to Figure It Out



Opening with runaway bride, Rachel Green at the altar, Friends encapsulates what it means to be figuring out life in the infamous "twentysomethings." Whether it be about love, frienships, family, or careers, watching Friends has it covered! As someone in their early twenities, this show is not only comforting, but relatable in so many ways. At the end of the day, aren't we all just trying to find a way to survive in the world?


Most importantly, Friends is about the importance of friendship. Sometimes when you're all alone out in the world, your friends become your family. It is about finding your "people" and sticking with them. They'll always be there for you and if you're lucky, you might even find your lobster!

Lobster Theory: "it's a known fact that lobsters fall in love and mate for life." - Phoebe Buffay






Characters:
Phoebe Buffay, Chandler Bing, Rachel Green, Ross Geller, Monica Geller, & Joey Tribbiani.
