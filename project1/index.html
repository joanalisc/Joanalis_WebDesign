The Rainbow Fish

The Rainbow Fish is the main character of the story. He is known for his beautiful shiny and rainbow scales.

Little Blue Fish

Little Blue Fish is one of the many fishes that admire Rainbow Fish for his shiny scales.

Starfish

Starfish is the character that listens to Rainbow Fish's troubles and directs him to the Wise Octopus.

Wise Octopus

The Wise Octopus is the character that helps Rainbow Fish acknowledge his mistakes and taches him a valuable lesson.

The Rainbow Fish lives in the deep blue waters where he is one of a kind - he has colorful and shiny scales.

While all of the other fish in the ocean wanted to play with him, he would always ignore them.

He was especially admired by little blue fish who followed him constantly and asked for one of his shiny scales, until the Rainbow Fish yelled at him to go away.

After this, no one wanted anything to do with the Rainbow Fish.

This made the Rainbow Fish sad. He didn't understand why he was so lonely if he had the most beautiful scales in the ocean.

He talked to the starfish about it, but the starfish couldn't help. However, he did tell him to see the Wise Octopus.

The Wise Octopus told him that if he gave a glitter scale to each of the fish, he would become happier and less lonely.

He was hesitant, but as soon as he gave one to little blue fish, seeing how happy he was made him happy!

He began to give away more - the more he did the happer he was.

Although he was left with one shiny scale, he was so happy to see others happy. He was no longer lonely and had many friends!
